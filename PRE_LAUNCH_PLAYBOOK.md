## METRICS TO TRACK DAILY

### Critical Nikita Bier Metrics:

**1. K-Factor (Virality Coefficient):**
```
K = (Number of invites sent per user) Ã— (Conversion rate of invites)

Target: K > 2.0
Gas achieved: K = 7+
```

**Track:**
- Average invites per user
- Invite conversion rate
- Overall K-factor

**If K < 1.5:** Your product won't grow virally
**If K > 2.0:** You're on track for viral explosion

**2. Time to First Share:**
```
Target: <60 seconds after signup
```

**Track:**
- Median time from signup to first share
- % of users who share within 1 minute

**If > 2 minutes:** Referral UX needs improvement

**3. Community Density:**
```
Target: >40% of specific community on platform
```

**Track per community:**
- Solana Discord: X / estimated_active_members
- CT: X / target_list_size
- friend.tech: X / top_creators

**If < 40%:** Don't expand to next community yet

**4. Launch Day Metrics:**
```
Target: >40% of waitlist active in first hour
```

**Track on March 15:**
- 4:00pm: How many opened email
- 4:00-5:00pm: How many logged in
- 4:00-6:00pm: How many shared
- 4:00-midnight: How many new signups from viral sharing

**5. Referral Conversion Funnel:**
```
Track:
- Referral link clicks
- Email signups from referrals
- Conversion rate per sharer
```

**Optimize for:**
- High click-through rate (>30%)
- High signup rate (>60% of clicks)

## EMERGENCY PLAYBOOK

### If K-Factor < 1.0 After 48 Hours:

**Diagnosis:**
- People aren't sharing
- Referral incentives too weak
- Sharing UX too complex

**Fixes:**
1. **Increase urgency:** "Only 20 spots left" (even if not true)
2. **Stronger rewards:** 1 referral = instant access (down from 3)
3. **Simplify sharing:** Remove all friction, one button
4. **Pre-fill everything:** Don't make users write anything
5. **Add leaderboard prizes:** Weekly $100 USDC to top referrer

### If Community Density < 20% After 1 Week:

**Diagnosis:**
- Wrong community target
- Not enough initial push
- Product-market fit issues

**Fixes:**
1. **Restart with different community:** Try friend.tech refugees instead
2. **Stronger initial push:** Partner with influencers
3. **Revisit messaging:** A/B test different pain points
4. **Manual outreach:** DM top 100 people in community directly

### If Launch Day Flops (<40% Active):

**Diagnosis:**
- Email went to spam
- Launch time wrong
- No coordinated push

**Fixes:**
1. **Re-launch:** Pick new date, 7 days later
2. **Manual notifications:** Twitter DMs, Discord pings, etc.
3. **Stronger FOMO:** "Last chance" messaging
4. **Influencer coordination:** Get 10+ influencers to tweet simultaneously
